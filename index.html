C.V. Raman Intermediate College

Welcome to C.V. Raman Intermediate College, an esteemed institution where academic excellence and holistic development have been the cornerstone since our inception in 2006

Named after the legendary physicist Dr C.V. Raman, our college has been a nurturing ground for young minds, encouraging them to excel in various fields, both academically and beyond.
